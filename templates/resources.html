Trevor Project

The Trevor Project is an American nonprofit organization founded in 1998. Focused on suicide prevention efforts among lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and questioning youth, they offer a toll-free telephone number where confidential assistance is provided by trained counselors.

Human Rights Campaign

The Human Rights Campaign (HRC) is an American LGBTQ advocacy group. The organization focuses on protecting and expanding rights for LGBTQ individuals, most notably advocating for same-sex marriage, anti-discrimination and hate crimes legislation, and HIV/AIDS advocacy.

Our Wave

Our Wave is an online platform for survivors to share stories, receive customized support, and join a community of understanding. Our Wave will provide survivors with the community and tools needed to share their voice and make an impact with their story.
